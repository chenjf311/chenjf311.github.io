Junfan Chen is an Associate Professor at the School of Software Engineering, Beihang University. He is also a member of the National Key Laboratory of Complex Critical Software Environment and the Beijing Advanced Innovation Center for Big Data and Brain Intelligence at Beihang University. He received his Ph.D. in Computer Science from Beihang University in January 2022, under the supervision of Professors Richong Zhang and Jie Xu. Subsequently, he conducted postdoctoral research at Beihang University under the guidance of Professor Chunming Hu and was appointed as an Associate Professor in May 2025.
His research focuses on natural language processing (NLP), knowledge engineering, and intelligent software engineering. He has made contributions to NLP methods with limited labeled data, including data augmentation and representation learning, sample selection and label denoising, and contrastive learning with adversarial optimization. He has published over 40 papers in top-tier international journals and conferences, such as TKDE, TOIS, ACL, KDD, WWW, AAAI, IJCAI, EMNLP, NAACL, CIKM, and COLING.
He actively serves as a program committee member or reviewer for prestigious venues including TKDE, TASLP, AAAI, ACL, KDD, and EMNLP. Additionally, he is a regular Area Chair for the Information Retrieval and Text Mining track of the ACL Rolling Review.
Dr. Chen has led several research projects, including the National Natural Science Foundation of China (NSFC) and a Postdoctoral Research Foundation project. He has also contributed as a sub-project leader for a National Key R&D Program of China (2030 Mega-Project) and participated in a Joint Fund Key Project.
